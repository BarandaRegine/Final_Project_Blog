Takoyaki Shop

Takoyaki is a round, fried food made from batter, small pieces of tender octopus and other ingredients, like green onions and ginger. They are considered a Japanese street food are popular at festivals, often prepared from food stands right in front of the prospective customers.Japanese culture has a significant influence here in our country, even their anime adaptations are a nostalgic reminder of our teenage years. Now, the growing trend here in the Philippines is the food that came originally from Japan.




Guides for Takoyaki Shop Business

Opening a takoyaki business has many different options, you can easily franchise an existing takoyaki business and they will provide all the things you need in opening this kind of business.

  • Prepare your Equipments


    • * Takoyaki Pan
    * Turning Spike
    * Oil Brush

  • Familiarize the Ingredients


    • * 1 2/3 cup flour Pan
    * 2 1/2 cup dashi soup
    * 2 eggs
    * 1/2 lb. boiled octopus, cut into bite-size pieces
    * 1/4 cup chopped green onion
    * 1/4 cup dried sakura ebi (red shrimp)
    * 1/4 cup chopped pickled red ginger

  • Offer Different Kind of Toppings


    • * Katsuobushi (dried bonito flakes)
    * Aonori (green seaweed powder)
    * Worcestershire sauce or takoyaki sauce
    * Mayonnaise

Tips for Takoyaki Shop Business

Takoyaki will open as a small food product business, over time the business will strive to expand. Innovations are also a goal for this food product business. As the success of the overall business increases, takoyaki will slowly expand to a wider range of locations. This expansion will allow Takoyaki to be accessed by a larger quantity of customers. The business will open more branches in areas nearby to the original.
