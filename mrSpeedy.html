Mr. Speedy Delivery Service

MRSPEEDY Philippines is one of Metro Manila's newest and fastest-growing same-day delivery service providers, ensuring convenient, dependable, and cost-effective services through its revolutionary online booking platform. It is the cheapest and quickest same-day delivery service in the Philippines, with excellent customer service. Money, wire transfer from your business account, or card-to-card transfer directly to the courier are all options for paying for express delivery. The cost is calculated inside the app. You can send to several addresses in one order with MrSpeedy. You can save more money with this feature. Only the best couriers are chosen for you, ensuring that you receive a reliable and responsible courier. You may place an order over the phone, online, or via an app without having to register.




Guide on How to Apply in Mr.Speedy

  • Process your Requirements

    • MrSpeedy is offering courier jobs in Philippines. We offer same day delivery service in Philippines to the local businesses, SME's and literally everyone. If you are in need of some money then you can work with us as a freelancer courier boy. This is a great opportunity to learn something new and earn money while doing it in your free time.

  • Download the App

    • UIf you want to apply to be a MrSpeedy partner rider, the first step is to download and install their mobile app for MrSpeedy courier partners – MrSpeedy: Courier & Driver Partner Jobs – from Google Play or Apple App Store (depending on your device).

  • Make a User Account

    • Fill in your personal details and choose from the available options to complete your app registration. Verify that all information is correct based on the records and documentation. The usual application fields and requirements is your: default navigator, full name, profile photo, driver's license, NBI or Police Clearance, LTO Registration, BIR Tax Identification Number, Vehicle Type, References and your Email.

  • Documentation Requirements should be uploaded.

    • You will be asked to upload soft copies of most, if not all, of the above-mentioned documents in addition to providing your personal information and other registration details.

  • Take the Orientation or the Online Examination (whenever still required)

    • MrSpeedy Philippines will perform face-to-face preparation and orientation (Mondays – Sundays, 10AM) for all rider applicants prior to the coronavirus pandemic. It was quickly replaced by an online test. Others, on the other hand, argue that this is no longer necessary. You can search for updates or contact their customer service department.

  • Pay the cash bond as well as the initial top-up fees (if it is still required)

    • MrSpeedy Philippines may have waived the initial sign-up fees, as well as the refundable cash bond (PHP500) and account top-up fees (PHP500), for a limited time . You can search for updates or contact their support team once more.

  • Await the approval.

    • While MrSpeedy Philippines claims that the application process for interested partner riders can take as little as five minutes (without visiting their office or using their mobile app) or a day (using the standard process), other applicants claim that they receive approval after four days or more, according to their official Facebook page.

  • Join the Riders' Facebook Page for the Most Up-to-Date Information.

    • For all interested applicants and partner riders, MrSpeedy Philippines maintains a Facebook page. To go to the list, search for: MrSpeedy Philippines for Courier Partners 

  • And Finally, you can now earn money as a MrSpeedy Partner Rider!
