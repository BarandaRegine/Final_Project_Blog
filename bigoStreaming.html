Guides On How To Earn Money From Streaming On BIGO

Unemployment is at an all-time high in many countries as a result of Covid-19, that is why Bigo Live aims to offer broadcasters and influencers a way to monetize their content across the web. The majority of users download BIGO LIVE and use it to watch live streaming and meet new people. On the other hand, some people use it to show off their skills and raise money. Earning money online may seem easy, but it is actually not, and that is the same for Bigo Live.

  • Work as a Host/Recruiter for BIGO LIVE

    • Bigo Live has an amazing opportunity for you to earn money by showcasing your talent to a global audience. Bigo Live is for you if you are an entertainer who enjoys engaging with people through your talent. You can also act as a Host or a Recruiter on Bigo Live. Bigo Live broadcasters have been known to receive up to $5,000 a month on a global scale. If you want to work as a recruiter, you will look for a host and earn a good income.

  • Earn by Encashing Virtual Gifts

    • Another way to make money is to cash in your virtual gifts. If you're a well-known streamer, your fans will give you virtual gifts. After that, you can stock up on these gifts that can be converted into ‘beans.' You will gain more beans the more famous you are.Make sure you're sharing some of your talents to ensure you get a large number of viewers, which is important for making money.

Tips To Earn Money From Streaming On BIGO

  • Go Live on a Regular Basis

    • You need to go live a lot if you want gifts that can be converted into money.  There are a lot of  new broadcasters go live 5-10 times in 1-3 minute intervals when you should be able to stay alive for more than a few minutes because how are aspiring managers and viewers will discover your broadcasts if you appear and vanish every few minutes?

  • Do something new

    • Do something unique to set yourself apart from other streamers. People were always interested in new things, so your live video must begin with it to attract more viewers.

  • Focus on the audience rather than the gifts.

    • Viewers give gifts for a variety of purposes and  “Because the broadcaster kept calling for diamonds,” was probably not on their list. Don't expect presents if you don't interact with your viewers. Make eye contact with your audience. Ask them questions or respond to them. When you engage your audience, they are more likely to want to help you.

  • Use Good Quality Phone

    • Make sure you're using a decent phone. If the quality of your live video is poor, you might expect a decrease in followers. Also, particularly if you're streaming music, pay attention to the audio quality.

  • Support Other Broadcasters

    • Find and join other influential broadcasters that you admire. Participate in their live broadcasts. Discover more about them. When you go live, some of them will follow you back and return the love.

  • Your broadcast should be scheduled and promoted.

    • The more people you can attract to watch ahead of time, the better. If you've got some great content to share, let people know when they should expect it. You can also use the social media accounts to share promotional materials.

  • Make a profile that is interesting.

    • Your profile picture is the first thing that will make a good impression on the viewer that is why you should make an effort to create an aesthetically pleasing profile image to gain more followers.
