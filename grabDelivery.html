Guides to be part of GrabFood Delivery Partner



  • Process your Requirements

    • According to Grab, here are the steps you have to first done to be part of their Grab Delivery Partner

    * Get or request for your LTO Professional Driver's License be updated to Professional

    * Schedule your appointment for NBI issuance or renewal

    * Take Drug Testing at any diagnostic clinics near you

    * Get your documents ready and proceed to sign up for validation

    * Complete and pass GrabFood Online Training

    * Make payment for Bag deposit fee

    * Bag/Starter Kit Collection

    * You can now start doing jobs

  • Must Have

    • These are the requirements we gathered base on Grab Website.

    * Possess smart phone with Appstore / Playstore app

    * No existing GrabFood / GrabExpress / GrabCar / GrabTaxi account

    * Does not have criminal records / require to pass background check

    * Not Suspended or Banned on Grab Platform

  • Age Qualification

    • In able to qualify to be part of Grab Delivery Partner. Applicants must be at the age of between 18 to 69 years old. A medical check up is required if you are above 50 years old

Tips to become GrabFood Delivery Partner



  • Be prepare at: 11am – 2pm and 4.30pm – 9pm

    • While it's not shocking that the best time to place orders is during mealtimes, it's still useful to know when it is for Singaporeans using the app. It's not that people don't order food after 9 p.m.; it's just that they don't arrive as quickly or as furiously as during the preceding hours.

  • Turn on the auto-accept to get more orders

    • You can choose whether or not to allow deliveries in regular mode. For auto-accept, it will be assumed that you will accept all orders, and Grab claims that riders in this mode will receive further orders.

  • Camp outside shopping centres for higher chances of getting an order

    • In an ideal world, you'd receive a new order as soon as you finished a delivery. The app, on the other hand, will normally send you orders based on your proximity to the restaurant. As a result, if you go to a shopping mall with a lot of food outlets, the chances of having an order are better.

  • Find your right Location

    • The general idea is that you should work in an area that you are familiar with or that is close to your home, particularly if you are doing this part-time. However, if you want to make more money, this might not be the best plan.

Pros & Cons of being GrabFood Delivery Partner

Pros of being in being GrabFood Delivery Partner

  • Flexible and timetable free work

    • You decide on how hard you will work on every single day. You may take a long break depens on you, but as for this kind of business, time always matter. You will be paid based on how you work hard for it.

  • You get paid based on your hardwork

    • Sometimes there are customers who are very generous. Aside from the income you will get for being a GrabFood Delivery Rider, you can also receive tips from your customers who appreciates your effort and hardwork.

Cons of being GrabFood Delivery Partner

  • Less control

    • You have less leverage over a customer's delivery experience whether you use an in-house or third-party fleet. Customers can have a negative opinion of your restaurant as a result of traffic, road conditions, or a wrong turn – delays that are unrelated to you or your food.

  • Irresponsible Customers

    • You will also encounter irresponsible customers. There are some who would order and later on will be cancelled. Some are also making fun and some are too mean. But you have to know how to deal with those kinds of customers that you will encounter later on.
