Lala Move Delivery Service

Lalamove fits in perfectly to disrupt and change the way deliveries are done in our tech-focused world, where anything can be done with just one click. We are a same-day delivery network that allows users to position orders through our app or website. We're revolutionizing the way people get things delivered while also making their lives easier. Almost anything you need can be delivered by us! From tiny packages to paperwork to furniture, we handle it all. We don't like playing with the law, of course! Illegal goods, poultry, and some types of bulky products, such as pianos, are all subject to prohibitions. If you're not sure if your item can be shipped, send a short message to our customer service team. Our team is available from 8 a.m. to 10 p.m. every day.

    • To become a Foodpanda delivery rider, you must first apply online. To get started, go to their website and select your location and car. Fill out the form with your personal information and upload images to prove that you meet the criteria mentioned above.
